About Pekline

Pekline Logistics is an independent Express Logistics provider based in Bedford specializing in express deliveries all over United Kingdom and main land Europe. Same day and next day express deliveries are our main expertise areas.

We run a wide variety of vehicles ranging from small 1 pallet vans, SWB, LWB XLWB, Luton Vans all the way up to 7.5 and 18tonners as well as 24t Artic Trailers. Dimensions and pictures of each of our vehicles can be found in the FLEET tab.

With our own fleet and the access to our partners’ vehicles availability is always guaranteed!
